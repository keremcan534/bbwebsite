About

This is an open community of independent developers, engineers, and digital artists. This platform is built as a collaborative space where members share knowledge, provide technical support, and help each other advance their individual projects. This website does not pursue any commercial interests and serves entirely as an informational hub for our community structure.

Foundation & Sustainability

To ensure the long-term financial sustainability of our ecosystem and support independent creators, BlackBlue Studios has officially registered as a non-profit foundation and legal corporate entity in the United States. While we remain a collaborative community at our core, this structural milestone will soon allow us to accept global donations and grants. These resources will be fully transparent and directly utilized to fund infrastructure, host community tools, and successfully publish upcoming independent projects developed within our network.
